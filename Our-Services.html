Our Services

Our work has spanned across diverse matters. We are registered with the Financial Services Board. We work in association with Multi-Net, PSG Consultants and Western Cape Board Centre in Providing excellent financial services to our clients.

Our firm complies with government's BEE policy and women perform significant and influential roles in the management thereof. We render both general and specialist legal and business services in the following area:

  • Conveyancing
  • Administration of Deceased Estates
  • Family Law
  • Road Accident Fund Claims
  • Debt Collecting
  • Rescission of Judgments
  • General Commercial Work
  • Liquidations
  • Administration of Insolvent Estates

F. Salie Attorneys is a member of the Black Conveyancers Association since 2006 and on all four of the major bank's panels, the Master's Liquidation Panel and the panel of the City of Cape Town, delivering work of high quality.

Fatima Salie

The firm is led by Fatima Salie, who has over seventeen years experience in the legal and commercial fraternity. In a distinguished career she has worked as:

  • Administrative Clerk (Administrator of deceased Estates, Formation of close corporations, formation of trusts) at Salie & Associates
  • Articled Clerk at J.M.B. Gillian Attorneys (2000-2001)
  • Articled Clerk at Alwie Lewis Attorneys (2001-2002)
  • Articled Clerk at Parker Holt Incorporated (2002-2003)
  • Attorney Conveyancer and Liquidator (2003-present)

Fatima is also registered with the Estate Agency Affairs Board, and is the principal of 62 On Long Street CC trading as SALPROP.
